liposuction

It is the process of removing the accumulated fats in some areas of the body through advanced medical techniques, and making the body attractive and consistent. Liposuction is performed for cosmetic purposes all over the world. However, it can be performed to treat some medical conditions and gynecomastia in men. Liposuction, also known as body sculpting, provides permanent solutions to reduce the number of fat cells that have grown as a result of being overweight. The liposuction process is usually performed on the following areas of the body: the abdomen area, buttocks, buttocks, arm, chest and back, chin and gland, waist, fat is removed from the body by a medical suction machine, to give the body a beautiful external appearance, besides that it is reduced Number of cells

Who can perform liposuction?

People over the age of 18, without health problems, and whose eligibility has been confirmed by doctors, can perform liposuction. It is also important that the patient does not have any problems with blood pressure and immune system. The doctor will examine the patient in detail and confirm his health. During the liposuction process, the duration of the procedure varies depending on the amount of liposuction and the technique used, but on average it takes 1-6 hours. The type of anesthesia is chosen according to the doctor's view, either general or local anesthesia. As is the case in all other cosmetic procedures, the doctor's experience is very important, especially in determining the amount of fat that will be aspirated and not to exceed 5 liters to maintain the patient's health.
